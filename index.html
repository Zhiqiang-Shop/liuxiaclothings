Our Policies & Guarantees

Shipping Policy

We deliver worldwide with a primary focus on Europe – UK, Germany, Spain, Portugal, and more. All orders are dispatched via premium air courier (DHL / FedEx / UPS) from our Guangzhou warehouse. Delivery time: 7-12 business days after shipment. Full tracking number provided instantly. You may also specify your preferred carrier. Customs duties & taxes (if any) are buyer's responsibility. We ensure secure packaging to avoid damage.

Returns & Refunds

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unused, unwashed, and original-tagged T-shirts. Buyer covers return shipping. Defective or wrong items: we reship or refund after inspection. Wholesale orders: please contact us for RMA number before returning. Refunds processed within 7 business days.

Privacy Policy

Your information is safe with us. We collect data only for order processing, shipment updates, and customer support. We never share or sell personal data to third parties. You may request data removal anytime. Our website uses SSL encryption for secure inquiries.
